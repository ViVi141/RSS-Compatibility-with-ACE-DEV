RSS × ACE-DEV Compatibility Layer

Bridges ACE-DEV medical states and real-time drug concentrations into Realistic Stamina System. Injuries drain stamina; treatment accelerates recovery. All effects computed from the ACE-DEV PK/PD system — no hardcoded timers, no RSS source modification.

=== FEATURES ===

Injury → Stamina Drain
• Bleeding: +0~40% stamina drain (oxygen carrying capacity loss)
• Pain: +0~20% stamina drain when moving (motor efficiency reduction)  
• Low Health: +0~30% stamina drain near death (systemic stress response)
• Endogenous Catecholamines: automatic compensation — ~95% at light injury, ~40% at critical

Medication → Stamina Effects (Real-time PK/PD Concentration Model)
• Epinephrine: concentration-proportional stamina recovery + metabolic rebound penalty on decline
• Morphine: pain suppression handled internally by ACE — bridge reads post-suppression pain values
• Metoprolol (β-blocker): +0~15% stamina drain when moving (limited cardiac output)
• Phenylephrine (vasopressor): +0~10% stamina drain (peripheral vasoconstriction)
• Naloxone (opioid antagonist): mild additional stamina drain (withdrawal stress)

Treatment → Stamina Recovery
• Bleeding stopped: stamina recovery bonus proportional to bleeding reduction
• Health restored: stamina recovery bonus proportional to health increase

=== DESIGN PRINCIPLES ===

• Zero modification to RSS or ACE-DEV source code — pure compatibility layer
• Energy expenditure rate model: ACE effects expressed as multipliers on RSS base drain rate, not fixed values
• Continuous PK/PD model: all drug effects computed from real-time blood concentration, naturally rising and metabolizing
• Graceful degradation: all ACE type references use Cast() with null checks — layer is silent when ACE is absent
• Affects all characters: players AND AI

=== HOW IT WORKS ===

ACE-DEV State                  Bridge Reads                 Stamina Impact
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Bleeding GetTotalBleedingAmount() → 0-1 continuous         Drain +0-40%
Pain GetPainIntensity()         → 0-1 (post-morphine)       Drain +0-20% (moving)
Health GetHealthScaled()        → 0-1 continuous            Drain +0-30%
Epinephrine PK conc. (nM)       → normalized 0-1             Recovery + decline penalty
Metoprolol PK conc. (nM)        → normalized 0-1             Drain +0-15% (moving)
Phenylephrine PK conc. (nM)     → normalized 0-1             Drain +0-10%
Naloxone PK conc. (nM)          → normalized 0-1             Minor additional drain
